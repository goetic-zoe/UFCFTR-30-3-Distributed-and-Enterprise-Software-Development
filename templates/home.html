Welcome to the Bristol Regional Food Network Marketplace

BRFN is a community initiative that aims to connect local food producers with consumers within a twenty-mile radius of Bristol. We currently provide our services to upwards forty small-to-medium enterprises that previously have had issues reaching customers beyond farmers markets and direct farm gate sales.

{# #}
Locally Sourced

All of our produce is locally sourced within 20 miles of Bristol to not just improve our suppliers experience but to also allow you, the customer, to gain access to the most fresh and ripe produce available.
