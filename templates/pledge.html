Our Commitment to Sustainability

Bristol Regional Food Network has pledged to keep sustainability at the forefront of our operations, making sure that we limit our carbon footprint at every step of your product's journey to you.

We work closely with local growers, producers, and suppliers to reduce food miles, support the regional economy, and ensure our food is as fresh as possible. By prioritising seasonal produce and sourcing from within the region wherever possible, we help minimise emissions associated with transportation while providing customers with high-quality food that reflects the best of what our local communities have to offer.

Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond sourcing. We are dedicated to reducing waste throughout our supply chain by encouraging responsible production methods, improving stock management, redistributing surplus food where possible, and promoting reusable, recyclable, or compostable packaging. We actively review our processes to identify opportunities for improvement, ensuring that we continue to reduce unnecessary waste and make the most efficient use of available resources.

Supporting environmentally responsible farming is another key part of our mission. We aim to work alongside producers who share our values, including those who adopt practices that protect biodiversity, improve soil health, conserve water, and minimise the use of harmful chemicals. Healthy ecosystems are essential for long-term food security, and we believe that sustainable agriculture benefits both the environment and future generations.

We also recognise that sustainability is about people as much as it is about the planet. By supporting independent farmers, local food businesses, and community initiatives, we help strengthen the regional economy and create opportunities for local employment. Building resilient local food systems means ensuring that producers receive fair value for their work while giving consumers access to affordable, high-quality food.

Education plays an important role in creating lasting change. We are committed to raising awareness of sustainable food choices by sharing information about seasonal produce, reducing household food waste, and encouraging responsible consumption. Through educational resources, community events, and partnerships with local organisations, we hope to inspire more people to make choices that have a positive impact on the environment.

Innovation is central to our long-term sustainability goals. As new technologies and environmentally friendly practices emerge, we will continue exploring ways to improve our operations, whether through cleaner transport options, more efficient logistics, renewable energy, or improved packaging solutions. We believe that continuous improvement is essential to building a food network that is fit for the future.

Transparency and accountability are equally important. We are committed to regularly reviewing our environmental performance, setting achievable sustainability goals, and working openly with our partners to identify areas where we can make meaningful progress. By measuring our impact and listening to feedback from our community, we can ensure that sustainability remains embedded in every aspect of our organisation.

Our vision is to create a regional food network that supports healthy communities, thriving local businesses, and a healthier environment. Every decision we make is guided by the belief that food should be produced, distributed, and consumed in ways that respect natural resources while strengthening the communities that depend on them.

Sustainability is not a destination but an ongoing journey. As we continue to grow, we remain committed to reducing our environmental impact, supporting local producers, embracing innovation, and working collaboratively to build a fairer, greener, and more resilient food system for Bristol and the surrounding region.
